Equal parts mountain hideout and fortress monastery, the [[Sigodo]] have a long tradition of residence here. First established by outcasts from [[Aspola]] for overtly worshipping [[Anzo]], the initial village eventually grew beyond its capacity to maintain its population, probably resulting in an exodus and the founding of [[Mara-Halyon]], though this is disputed. 

In any case, the location quickly became depopulated and a hardening of religious views began. This culminated in hostility between the rulers of [[Mara-Halyon]] and the [[Sigodo]], necessitating the construction of fortifications. The eventual and suspicious demise of [[Mara-Halyon]]'s Archon, however, meant that open hostilities never broke out.
