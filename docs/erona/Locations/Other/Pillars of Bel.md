In the far south-western seas, the [[Altom Ocean]] lie the pillars of [[Bel]]. If the tall tales spun by sailors are to be believed, the [[Pillars of Bel]] are vast waterfalls cast from high in the heavens, rushing down into the ocean from unknowable heights. They are believed to be [[Bel]]'s connection to his watery domain from the heavens where he resides, and some swear they have seen the god travel within those watery roads.
