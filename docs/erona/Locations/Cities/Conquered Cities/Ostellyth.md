Formerly Ostellos, Ostellyth is the capital of the kingdom of [[Thalendor]] and the largest city in northern [[Osera]]. 

It is ruled by the Aldrician dynasty, founded by the great king Aldric who led his people from the [[Unsung Lands]] to Osera. 

The [[Voyage of Normund]] was begun from Ostellyth.
