Formerly known as Qorai, [[Koray]] was looted during the [[Voyage of Normund]] and left to [[Brethiln]] of [[Mirenia]] to inhabit. Most of its [[Cenerian]] colonists fled or began accommodating their new [[Brethiln]] overlords. Nevertheless, [[Koray]] has always had great [[Brethiln]] influences, and its new rulers had a de jure presence in the city for centuries.
