Largest city in the [[Cenerian]] north of [[Osera]] and owner of the second-largest navy in the [[Cenerian League]] (the first being [[Pallas]]), known for its successful defence during the [[Voyage of Normund]], holding out over several years.

![[enetia.png]]

#### Founding
Enetia was founded as a small trading outpost during the [[Mycean]] Age, intended to trade along what was then a large river flowing into [[Halcyon]]. After the flood, Enetians had to confront their new oceanic environment which now surrounded them on all sides. Their new lagoon allowed them to build a flourishing port city, safe from the [[Horrors]] of the mainland.
