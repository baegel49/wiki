One of the last remaining [[Cenerian League]] aligned city on [[Zinnabar]] (the other being [[Mara-Halyon]]), and the first founded there by the [[Mycean]]s. Its inhabitants are nominally [[Cenerian]] but often still refer to themselves as [[Mycean]], while others refer to them as [[Southern]]. 

#### Name
Aspola is often called the city of bronze for its long history of its bronze forges which combined the local tin with copper from abroad. This reputation was reinforced when Archon Ennevon began refurbishing the city's domes with copper.

#### Quarters
Like most [[Cenerian]] cities, [[Aspola]] has an Acropolis, situated on the aptly named Acropolis Hill. Opposite the Acropolis lies the Nyx hill, on which assemblies of citizens were held in the past, which now happen in the Towers quarter ringing the Acropolis hill instead. Between the two hills lies the Agora, home to the temple of Luminor, a stadium, and the public assembly. The isles, a network of channels and canals, connect to the Agora. Jutting out into the sea is the semi-abandoned old quarter, originally known as First Landing, now often called the 'Rookery'. Lastly, the [[Qedhi]] quarter exists on the third hill which the walls of Aspola surround.

#### Sites
Aspola is home to the largest temple of [[Luminor]], situated square in between the two hills of the city.
