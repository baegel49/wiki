A city in the [[Valkyros]] region which has sworn nominal allegiance to the [[Cenerian League]]. However, the city is internally divided. On its throne sits 'Arkon' Grag, an [[Altani]] mercenary-turned-conqueror who dethroned the previous Archon Enkono after he refused to pay Grag's warband. Further, a group of [[Thalen]] soldiers and their families have settled across the river after a failed siege (foiled by Grag's warband). They have now come to a truce with Grag as the new ruler, and have begun turning their siege camp into a makeshift settlement called Ondervar (lit. 'Other/New Var{anth}).
