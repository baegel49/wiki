The [[Akkron Wall]], sometimes simply the 'Akkron', is the main dividing mountain range between [[Osera]] and the [[Karrow]] as well as the rest of the east, such as the [[Unsung Lands]]. 

It was first discovered by [[Mycean]] explorers and colonists, who in turn met the [[Akkurim]] which inhabited the range and the lands now known as the [[Karrow]]. Today, the [[Akkron Wall]] is the primary defence against creatures from the eastern wastelands and is patrolled by the [[Dunwarden]].

The [[Akkron Wall|Wall]] is home to the [[Manlings]] city of [[Bal-Gorund]], as well as several [[Altani]] encampments. Mountain Men also inhabit the range, as well as some [[Skrats]]. Further, the finest and largest [[Rykes]] make their nests here.
