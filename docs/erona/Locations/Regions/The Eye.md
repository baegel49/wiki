A massive impact crater in the [[Sea of Alvos]] between [[Osera]] and [[Hesperon]] in what used to be northern [[Halcyon]]. 

#### Origin
[[The Eye]] was created when the largest shard of the falling star that caused the [[Collapse]] impacted northern [[Halcyon]]. The sages of the [[Seventh Sanctum]] are still stupefied by the sheer size of the crater, calculating that, at its size, nothing should have remained of [[Erona]] at all. 

#### Resources
[[The Eye]] is the single greatest source of concentrated [[Haze]] which can best be brewed into the well-known street drug known by the same name. [[Enetia]] has an effective monopoly over the region, but has also begun exploring [[Hesperon]]'s craters for similar concentrations with some success.
