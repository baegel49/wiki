The sea forming the new divide between [[Osera]] and [[Hespenor]] after the central part of [[Halcyon]] experienced near-endless flooding. The Sea of Alvos is named after the Sovereign of the [[Mycean]] people who ruled during the [[Collapse]]. The sea itself is quite shallow, particularly in formerly hilly and mountainous areas, requiring deceptive amount of skill to navigate. Those now often form island chains, still named after their former peaks.
