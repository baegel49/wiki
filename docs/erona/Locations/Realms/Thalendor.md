For the first time in their history, the [[Thalen]] people are united. After securing both the [[Thalen Migration|migration]] and victory over the [[Brethiln]], there was little doubt that [[Aldric]] would unite his people under one banner, and so he did. What was left out of that story was that the bureaucratic might of the [[Sect of Pyos]] allowed [[Aldric]] to codify his people's laws and institutionally rule them. Now, the [[Thalen]] are the dominant culture in central [[Osera]], and [[Aldric]]'s son [[Normund]] rules with an iron fist.



Kingdom of the [[Thalen]], ruled by the Aldrician dynasty. Capital is [[Ostellyth]]. 

The kingdom is divided into great holdings:

Central lands: 

- Ecardun - ruled by [[Ecard]]
- Crownlands - ruled directly by [[Normund]]
- Highkeep - ruled by Merovin
- Northwatch - ruled by Balghin
- Falcgard - ruled by Falcor II

Eastern lands: 

- Osmark - ruled by Siggort
- Dinven - ruled by Bathert
- Lanvar - ruled by Brennock

Southern lands: 

- Corryn - ruled by Theodmar
- Lythmark - ruled by Norvan
- Averlan - ruled by Sigholt
