Kingdom of the [[Thalen]], ruled by the Aldrician dynasty. Capital is [[Ostellyth]]. 

The kingdom is divided into great holdings:

Central lands: 

- Ecardun - ruled by [[Ecard]]
- Crownlands - ruled directly by [[Normund]]
- Highkeep - ruled by Merovin
- Northwatch - ruled by Balghin
- Folcgard - ruled by Folcen II

Eastern lands: 

- Osmark - ruled by Siggort
- Dinven - ruled by Bathert
- Lanvar - ruled by Brennock

Southern lands: 

- Corryn - ruled by Theodmar
- Lythmark - ruled by Norvan
- Averlan - ruled by Sigholt
