The southern realm of [[Osera]], inhabited by the [[Cenerian]] people. [[Ceneria]] is a wider realm divided into smaller realms, each centred around an initial [[Mycean]] colony. As such, the realms themselves are less important than their capitals, which command most the population within their walls.
