The Sigodo are an infamous order of assassins that reside within their mountain fortress near [[Mara-Halyon]] on [[Zinnabar]]. They are prolific users (and importers) of [[Haze]] and believe that using it while performing a kill results in entering an enlightened state. They are overt worshippers of [[Anzo]], a fact that makes employing them difficult and has invited *[[Cenerian|nemesis]]* for those patrons that were caught hiring them. 

It is rumoured that [[Andros]] used the Sigodo's reputation to coerce some Archons into supporting his coronation during the [[Crowning of Andros]].
