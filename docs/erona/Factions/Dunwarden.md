The [[Dunwarden]] are an ancient order of rangers which patrol the [[Akkron Wall]]. They swear no loyalty to any ruler nor god, and keep mostly to themselves. 

#### Origin
The etymology of the [[Dunwarden]] name suggests a [[Brethiln]] origin, and indeed, the [[Brethiln]] have a history of an ancient tribe which lived and presumably patrolled the [[Akkron Wall]]. History turns to myth here, though there are suggestions that this tribe was eventually hired by the [[Akkurim]] to continue their patrols along the mountain range, presumably to defend against threats from the west. After [[Mycean]] victory over the [[Akkurim]], said tribe likely switched sides, quite literally, to patrol the same mountains against threats from the east. This, so far, traces the most likely origin. 

#### Organisation
The [[Dunwarden]] are organised around patrols and base camps, the locations of which are known only to them. They have a strong presence in [[Naporia]] and [[Linov]], which act as northern and southern axes of organisation and recruitment. 

#### Recruitment
The [[Dunwarden]] are a politically agnostic order, taking in members from any social strata, religion, and creed. Their primary method of recruitment is by offering sanctuary to persecuted peoples, such as criminals and traitors.
