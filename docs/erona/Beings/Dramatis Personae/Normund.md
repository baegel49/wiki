[[Normund]] is the grandson of the great [[Thalen]] king Aldric who led his kindred across the [[Karrow]] from the [[Unsung Lands]] and into contemporary [[Brethil]], now [[Thalendor]]. [[Normund]] is now the current king of [[Thalendor]], who made his name with his invasion of [[Ceneria]], known as the [[Voyage of Normund]]. He was crowned after [[Aldric]]'s sons murdered each other in the [[Sons' War]].
