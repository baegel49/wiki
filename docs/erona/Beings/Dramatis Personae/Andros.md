[[Andros]] is the Archon of [[Pallas]] and Sovereign of the [[Cenerian League]], though he claims to be Sovereign of 'all known lands'. He is [[Cenerian]] born and claims dynastic lineage to [[Mycean]] nobility, most famously [[Alvos]] himself. 

The [[Crowning of Andros]] was the confluence of his political power, happening when he was 24 years old as a response to the [[Voyage of Normund]] and [[Xeril's Conquests]]. 

Andros has succeeded in signing a peace treaty with [[Normund]] and has dedicated the years since his coronation to defeating [[Xeril]] in south-eastern [[Ceneria]] and [[Zinnabar]] with a combined force of [[Cenerian]] soldiers and [[Altani]] mercenaries. This is known as the [[War of Recovery]].
