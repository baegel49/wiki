

Skrats are swamp and bog dwelling humanoids, not much larger than a child. They are, however, extremely strong for their size, on par with a late teenager, and can inflict serious wounds on unsuspecting travellers and undefended hamlets. 

Snivelling, feral, mindless, anarchic, and uncivilised are regular words used for Skrats. Nevertheless, Skrats do have a very (very) basic level of culture, mostly involving the survival of their inhospitable living conditions through invading, looting, and killing their surroundings, or each other.
