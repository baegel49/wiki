God (with human origin) of oaths, laws, and civilisation, represented by an eye. Worshipped by the [[Sect of Pyos]]. Originally worshipped by [[Mycean]]s, then [[Cenerian]]s, and now popular among the [[Thalen]]. 

Pyos is believed to have founded the ancient city of [[Mycea]] and ensuring its dominance by giving its people the knowledge of [[Arcanum]].
