[[Brethiln]] deity that presides over all land, life, believed to be at home in the mythical 'World Tree' the location of which has been lost to time. In [[Brethiln]] lore, the '[[Sky-Mother]]' is his wife, and all beings are their children. Interestingly, the [[Rurii]] also worship a (potentially identical) being who they also call the [[Sky-Mother]].
