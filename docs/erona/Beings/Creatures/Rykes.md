Rykes are extremely large raptors that stalk the skies of the [[Karrow]]. They are the symbol of [[Thalendor]] and its Aldrician dynasty ever since they adopted them as hunting birds during their great migration from the [[Unsung Lands]]. 

A Ryke has a wingspan of 4 metres, and usually weighs the same as a small hunting hound. They are infamous for attacking and carrying off small livestock, pets, and even children.

Rykes are, however, marginally intelligent animals, able to be trained but not thoroughly domesticated. They are wilful and headstrong but can understand that cooperating with their handler will net them stable access to food. 

Those who keep Rykes are known as Rykers, and are often trusted individuals in [[Thalen]] courts.
