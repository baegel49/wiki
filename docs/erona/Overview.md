The world of [[Erona]] is mired in forgotten myths and hazardous lands scarred by cataclysm, the most recent known to its scholars as the [[Collapse]]. Those who survived have slowly rediscovered their past and rebuilt what they could. The Known World is now divided into three continents: [[Osera]], home of the [[Cenerian League]] and the Grand Kingdom of [[Thalendor]]; [[Hespenor]], the western remnant and doomed continent with only one surviving city, [[Karsolam]]; and [[Zinnabar]], the southern continent least affected by the [[Collapse]]. 

The [[Collapse]] that destroyed the [[First League]] and [[Mycea]] has left its mark. Its residue is the [[Haze]], a corrupting ether that pools in the woods and mountains of [[Osera]] and [[Hespenor]], and the [[Horrors]] that hunt inside it are at home there in a way that men are not. East of [[Osera]] lies the [[Karrow]], a wasteland steeped permanently in [[Haze]], kept at arm's length by the [[Akkron Wall]] and the [[Dunwarden|rangers]] who walk it. Most of what the [[Mycean]]s knew went down with them. The sages of the [[Seventh Sanctum]], seventh of its name and the first to last, spend their lives recovering fragments of it, and the [[Sect of Pyos]] rules on what those fragments are permitted to mean.

The present age belongs to the [[Cenerian League]], a confederation of city-polities that trade, scheme, and occasionally murder one another, and which submits to a single Sovereign only when the alternative is worse. That Sovereign is [[Andros]], at war in the south with the [[Rurii]] warlord [[Xeril]], who took three League cities and rules them still. In the north, the [[Thalen]] hold what its migrants took from the [[Brethiln]] two generations ago, and its old [[Normund|king]] watches the League for weakness. Beyond the edges of the map lie the [[Unsung Lands]], the [[Empire of Kantara]], and a great deal that no one has yet written down.
