The world of [[Erona]] is mired in forgotten myths and hazardous lands scarred by cataclysm, the most recent known to its scholars as the [[Collapse]]. Those who survived have slowly rediscovered their past and rebuilt what they could. The Known World is now divided into three continents: [[Osera]], home of the [[Cenerian League]] and the Grand Kingdom of [[Thalendor]]; [[Hespenor]], the western remnant and doomed continent with only one surviving city, [[Karsolam]]; and [[Zinnabar]], the southern continent least affected by the [[Collapse]] where the [[War of Recovery]] rages.

The [[Collapse]] that destroyed the [[First League]] and [[Mycea]] has left its mark. Its residue is the [[Haze]], a corrupting ether that pools in the woods and mountains of [[Osera]] and [[Hespenor]], and the [[Horrors]] that hunt inside it are at home there in a way that men are not. East of [[Osera]] lies the [[Karrow]], a wasteland steeped permanently in [[Haze]], kept at arm's length by the [[Akkron Wall]] and the [[Dunwarden|rangers]] who walk it. Most of what the [[Mycean]]s knew went down with them. The sages of the [[Seventh Sanctum]] spend their lives recovering fragments of it. The [[Sect of Pyos]] and [[Sect of Illumination]] keep their own histories, convinced that their religions are the way forward.

The present age loosely belongs to the [[Cenerian League]], a confederation of city-states that trade, scheme, and occasionally murder one another, and who have submitted to a single dictator, known as Sovereign, due to times of crisis. That Sovereign is [[Andros]], at war in the south with the [[Rurii]] warlord [[Xeril]], who took three League cities and rules them still. In the north, the [[Thalen]] hold what its migrants took from the [[Brethiln]] two generations ago, and its old [[Normund|king]] watches the League for weakness. Beyond the edges of the map lie the [[Unsung Lands]], the [[Empire of Kantara]], and a great deal that no one has yet written down or seen.
