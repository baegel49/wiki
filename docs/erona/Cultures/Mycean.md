The original but now #extinct human culture of [[Halcyon]] which dominated the continent. Artefacts and ruins survive their rule, though many lie at the bottom of the [[Sea of Alvos]], including the capital, [[Mycea]]. 

Mycea itself was the jewel of the [[First League]] and its de facto leading polity. The Archons of Mycea styled themselves 'Sovereigns' of the League, and were rarely challenged over the eight hundred years of the League's existence. 

The Myceans are often rumoured to have been responsible for creating the [[Wardens]], but this remains conjecture.
