The [[Qedhi]] are a scattered people, whose diaspora live in many of the originally [[Mycean]], now [[Cenerian]], cities. They were one of the many conquered peoples subsumed by the [[First League]] and proved adaptable enough to the [[Mycean]] way of life to inhabit their own quarters within the city walls of the various colonies.  

The [[Qedhi]] relationship with their conquerors was not always harmonious. Over the centuries slave uprisings, xenophobia, and misunderstandings often boiled over. In some cities, the entire [[Qedhi]] population was murdered, in others they fled.
