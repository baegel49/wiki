The Altani are giants inhabiting many mountain ranges across [[Osera]]. They often stand at seven or eight feet tall, and have strong builds due to a diet of meat and milk. They are androgynous, and only reproduce once in an individual's lifetime. Their skins range from swarthy to grey to pale. 

> "It is curious that the beings we call Altani are called 'Children of the [[Titans]]' in [[Rurii]] speak. They seem to have accepted that the Altani, to them at least, are the descendants of the fabled bridge builders."
