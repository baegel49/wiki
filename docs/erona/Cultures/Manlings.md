The [[Manlings]] are a shorter breed of humans, primarily found in [[Bal-Gorund]], their main city. They are sometimes, derogatorily, referred to as 'Dwarves' or 'Halflings'. They are known for their underground structures which tunnel through the largest peak of the [[Akkron Wall]], Mount Watcher. They are incredibly secretive, and no living outsiders are known to have entered [[Bal-Gorund]]. 

#### Origin
It is unclear whether Manlings predate or derive from humans. There are next to no sources mentioning 'Manlings' specifically, making research about them next to impossible. Their own sages are not forthcoming with information, either. 

#### Interactions with other cultures
It has become a fashion in [[Ceneria]] to have at least one Manling on an Archon's council, both for their insights but also their uncanny ability in [[Arcanum]]. The kings and barons of [[Thalendor]] have adopted this system as well. Sometimes these [[Manlings]] are employed when rare trading convoys reach these rulers, other times they forcibly recruited.
