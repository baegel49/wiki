
#### Central Tension


#### Politics
###### Brethil-Thalen Conflict
The original war between the [[Brethiln]] and [[Thalen]] peoples has largely subsided. What remains are border disputes and the slowly encroaching [[Thalen]] settlers on [[Brethil]]. This simmering tension has slowly been building up again due to the [[Thalen]] with the [[Cenerian League]] and lack of expansion options for its booming population. 

###### Thalen-Cenerian Conflict
Though the peace has been signed in [[Arcanum]], not everything is rosy. [[Cenerian]] priests of the [[Sect of Pyos]] are outraged that [[Thalen]] priests are now allowed in the city, and the recent election of the first [[Thalen]] Patriarch has nearly resulted, and may still result, in a schism. Beyond the religious conflict, [[Thalen]] pirates hound [[Enetia]]n merchantmen and the [[Golden Bay]].
