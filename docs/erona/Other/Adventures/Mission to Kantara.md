From [[Adventure Starts]]. 

### Overview
The council of [[Pallas]] has tasked a merchant admiral with escorting diplomats and trade goods to the [[Empire of Kantara]]. For this purpose, one ship and two escort vessels will set sail from [[Pallas]], make berth at [[Sydon]] (where the charts to Kantara need to be acquired) and set sail for the famed Bejewelled City, home of the Pearlescent Emperor. 

Note: The players can take the role of the merchant admiral, diplomats, or the marines on-board the ship. Virtually any background or class can make an appearance.

#### Roles

- Master - master of the ship, akin to captain
- Pilot - master of the helm
- Navigator - tracks maps and keeps to the bow of the ship to read the waves
- Master of the Watch - calls watches, sets rhythm of work, responsible for repairs
- Scribe - keeps the ledger, responsible for rations and water
- Marine Commander - responsible for the marines and their upkeep
