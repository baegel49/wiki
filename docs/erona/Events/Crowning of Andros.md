The crowning of [[Andros]] was a momentous occasion which marked the ascent of Andros as 'sovereign', a title not used since [[Mycean]] days. 

Andros used the crowning, attended by nearly all Archons of the [[Cenerian League]], to unite them under his banner. 

The crowning was a reaction to the dual threat of the [[Voyage of Normund]] and [[Xeril's Conquests]], which threatened [[Cenerian]] existence.

It is rumoured that [[Andros]] used the [[Sigodo]]'s reputation to coerce some Archons into supporting his coronation during the [[Crowning of Andros]].
