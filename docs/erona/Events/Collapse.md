A cataclysmic event of earthquakes, floods, volcanic eruptions, and asteroid strikes that shook [[Erona]]. It split the original continent of [[Halcyon]] in twain, leaving behind [[Osera]] and [[Hesperon]].

Except for the largest peaks, most of [[Halcyon]] was drowned during the flood. Some sages predicted this collapse, and the second, third, and fourth Sanctums were moved to these peaks, now known as the [[Barrier Islands]].
