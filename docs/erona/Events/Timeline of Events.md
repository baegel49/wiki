Erona has had, according to Mycean reckoning, three distinct ages, each of which began with a calamity that befell the world. The first age, the Fabled Age, has no real timeline of events, being steeped in myth and legend. It ended in the God-War, a fabled battle where two human demi-gods and two primordial gods (Pyos, Luminor, Bel, Anzo) rose against the other gods for supremacy of the heavens, and won, leaving the world to mortals. 

#### The Mycean Age

| Year            | Event                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0 M.A.          | The mythical founding of [[Mycea]], supposedly by Pyos after the God-War as a departing gift to mortals                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ~150 M.A.       | Founding of the [[First League]] as a check against a growing, rival league, the two locked into bitter conflict for decades, followed by peace, only to be followed by war again.                                                                                                                    |
| ~600 M.A.       | Mycean colonisation of eastern [[Halcyon]] begins by sea. The region will eventually become [[Ceneria]].                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| ~750-~1250 M.A. | Peak [[Mycean]] dominance after uniting all of Halcyon under the League against outside threats. [[Heavenly Iron]] forging begins, [[Arcanum]] is codified in the [[First Sanctum]].                                                                                                                  |
| 1449 M.A.       | The [[Collapse]]. Earthquakes, floods, eruptions, asteroid strikes. Halcyon splits in two: [[Osera]] in the east, [[Hesperon]] in the west. Mycea destroyed; most First League cities destroyed. [[Karsolam]] alone survives in Hesperon, isolated. The Mycean culture, as a continuous polity, ends. |

#### The Dark Age

| Year             | Event                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0 D.A. - 81 D.A. | [[Erona]] reels under the effects of the Collapse. Survivors regroup on Osera, now the eastern half of former Halcyon, which was least affected by the flood due to its rugged landscape. The days are dark and grey, and soot falls from the sky, earning these 80 odd years the nomer of Dark Age. [[Mist]] first begins to appear. The 6th Sanctum is founded and burned as scapegoats for the Collapse. |
