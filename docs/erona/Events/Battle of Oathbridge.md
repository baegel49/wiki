The Battle of Oathbridge was a decisive battle between the clans of the [[Brethiln]] led by [[Rogan]]and the army of [[Thalen]] led by [[Aldric]]. The result was a decisive victory for the Thalen, who used the battle and subsequent rout of the Brethiln to demarcate their northern borders of [[Thalendor]]. It was during this battle that [[Aldric]] converted to the [[Sect of Pyos]], after seeing the sign (an eye) of Pyos in the sky illuminating the Brethiln armies.
