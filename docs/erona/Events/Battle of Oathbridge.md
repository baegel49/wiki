The Battle of Oathbridge was a decisive battle between the clans of the [[Brethiln]] led by High Chief [[Rogan]] and the army led by the [[Thalen]] king [[Aldric]]. The result was an overwhelming victory for the [[Thalen]], who used the battle and subsequent rout of the [[Brethiln]] to demarcate their northern borders of [[Thalendor]]. 

It was before and during this battle that [[Aldric]] converted to the [[Sect of Pyos]], after supposedly seeing the sign (an eye) of [[Pyos]] in the sky illuminating the Brethiln armies, allowing him to predict their movement and counter it. [[Aldric]] died soon after but his conversion was recorded by his high advisor and personal priest. The battle was fought at an (to the Thalen) unnamed bridge, which they henceforth named Oathbridge, to honour [[Aldric]]'s oath to the god of truth. 

Some Thalen disputed that [[Aldric]] ever converted, blaming a biased history written by a Pyosian priest who used the events of the battle in favour of the [[Sect of Pyos]]. Nevertheless, many Thalen nobles followed suit in converting, including [[Aldric]]'s eventual successor, [[Normund]].
