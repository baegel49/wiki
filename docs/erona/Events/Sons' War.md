A succession crisis and civil war between the sons of [[Aldric]] which resulted in the crowning of [[Normund]], son of [[Athelric]] and first grandson of [[Aldric]], after defeating his uncle [[Falcor]] at the [[Siege of Falcgard]]

[[Normund]] was very young at the time, and won thanks to the aid of the great [[Thalen]] commander and castellan [[Ecard]], son Ecard.
