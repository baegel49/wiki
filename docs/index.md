# Erona

Erona is a world of three continents — **Osera**, **Hesperon**, and **Zinnabar** — divided by the shallow, drowned **Sea of Alvos**. Unlike our world it has no moon and the nights are dark.

The shape of the present is set by one event: the **[Collapse](erona/Events/Collapse.md)**, a cataclysm that tore the supercontinent of Halcyon in two, ended the Mycean age, leaving the survivors to rebuild from islands and ruins.

## The Known World

**[Osera](erona/Locations/Regions/Osera.md)** is the eastern remnant of Halcyon, divided between three realms: **[Ceneria](erona/Locations/Realms/Ceneria.md)** in the south, the kingdom of **[Thalendor](erona/Locations/Realms/Thalendor.md)** at its centre, and the shrinking realm of **[Brethil](erona/Locations/Realms/Brethil.md)** to the north. **[Hesperon](erona/Locations/Regions/Hesperon.md)**, the western remnant, was broken by the Collapse and is stalked by Horrors; only **[Karsolam](erona/Locations/Cities/League%20Cities/Karsolam.md)** still stands there. To the south lies **[Zinnabar](erona/Locations/Regions/Zinnabar.md)**, a dry continent that has become contested ground between the Cenerian League and the Rurii conquests.

Beyond the maps lie the **[Karrow](erona/Locations/Regions/Karrow.md)**, the **[Unsung Lands](erona/Locations/Regions/Unsung%20Lands.md)**, and the **[Far South East](erona/Locations/Regions/Far%20South%20East.md)**.

## Powers of the Age

The **[Cenerian League](erona/Factions/Cenerian%20League.md)** are heirs to the  First League and holds southern Osera. The **[Aldrician kings of Thalendor](erona/Locations/Realms/Thalendor.md)** hold the centre. The **[Rurii](erona/Cultures/Rurii.md)** push from the east under the conquests of Xeril. The **[Sect of Pyos](erona/Factions/Sect%20of%20Pyos.md)** binds oaths and laws across both realms; the **[Seventh Sanctum](erona/Factions/Seventh%20Sanctum.md)** keeps what knowledge survived.

## Dating

This wiki uses the Mycean reckoning of three ages. The **Mycean Age** (M.A.) runs from 0 to 1449, ending in the Collapse. The **Dark Age** (D.A.) follows, lasting 81 years. The **Cenerian Age** (C.A.) begins after that and is the current age. Present day is **155 C.A.**
